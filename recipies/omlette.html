Omlette

The versitale omlette

Once you understand the basics there is so many ingredients you can experiment with.

  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 c cheddar cheese
  • 2 T cream
  • 1/4 c cubed ham
  • 1/2t butter
  1. crack eggs into bowl with cream and whisk till frothy
  2. melt butter in medium heat pan
  3. pour egg mixture into pan and let cook briefly
  4. add remaining ingredients
  5. cook till center is set, fold in half and serve
