Ramen

A tasty meal for a cold day

With a small amount of effort you can really improve the basic cheap ramen.

  • package of ramen
  • 1 egg
  • 1 T sriracha
  • 1 T peanutbutter
  • 2 c water
  1. Bring water to a boil, reduce to simmer
  2. Add seasoning packet, sriracha and peanut butter stir till disolved
  3. gently crack egg into water
  4. after 1 minute add ramen being careful not to break egg
  5. cook about 2-3 min until ramen is soft
  6. pour into bowl and enjoy
